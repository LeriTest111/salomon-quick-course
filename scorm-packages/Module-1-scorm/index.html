Brand positioning

Premium, innovative, authentic

The line to remember

We are the most premium, innovative and authentic brand for the mountain sport lifestyle

Premium

The shoes are built for performance and comfort, using quality materials that last longer and perform better in tough conditions. Customers are paying for reliability, comfort and confidence, not just a name.

Innovative

This shoe creates solutions to real running and outdoor problems. This includes better grip on wet or uneven surfaces, more comfort over long distances, and a more secure fit.

Authentic

This shoe comes from real mountain sport experience. The products are shaped by athletes, designers and engineers who test them in real outdoor environments, not just in a lab.
